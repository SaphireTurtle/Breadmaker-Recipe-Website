    <div><h2>The Recipe: Breadmaker Sweet Bread</h2></div>


    <hr>
    
    <h3><u>Ingredients</u></h3>


    <div>
      <ul>
        <li>1 cup and 2 Tbsp of Water</li>
        <li>1.5 Tbsp butter</li>
        <li>3 cups of All-Purpose flour</li>
        <li>1 and 1/2 teaspoons of yeast</li>
        <li>3 Tbsp Sugar</li>
        <li>3/4 tsp salt</li>
      </ul>
    </div>


    <h3><u>Instructions</u></h3>


    <div>
      <ol>
        <li>Add the water to the breadmaker basket</li>
        <li>Add the butter.</li>
        <li>Add the flour</li>
        <li>Add the rest of the dry ingredients</li>
        <li>Choose the normal bread setting on your breadmaker. Make sure it is on 2lb loaf setting and medium crust. Press start, and let the breadmaker do it's magic!</li>
        <li>Take it out and enjoy!</li>
      </ol>
